About

Anachebe Asomugha is a web development student, earning to develop web applications at Bellevue University. He was born in Evansville, a city in southern Indiana that is situated on the Ohio River that also has the pleasure of being the 249th most populous city in the nation, as of the 2020 census. When Anachebe is not studying to become a web developer, he is acting, singing, producing music, or playing video games.

Acting on the stage is among this favorite things to do. He is enamored with the camaraderie involved in bringing a script to life with a group of his peers, live in front of an audience. There is something about the fact that, in live performance, anything can happen and it would exist in the memory of the people there, then the performance ends and everyone goes home only to come back and repeat it again the next night.
